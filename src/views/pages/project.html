Project

Best Start in Life - Improving outcomes for children aged 0-5

January 2019 - August 2020

Status: Active

Details

Cambridgeshire and Peterborough councils are partnering with public health, CCG and other organisations to create a joint strategy and integrated delivery model to improve outcomes for children from pre-birth to age 5. The strategy is in it's final phases of development and the integrated delivery model will be shaped over the coming months. This will result in a joint programme of work, redesigning services around user need.
